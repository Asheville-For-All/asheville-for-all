Thank You for Supporting the Tourism Taxes for Affordable Housing Campaign

Thanks to everyone that showed up at the start of the month to help the Tourism Taxes for Affordable Housing coalition—which includes Asheville DSA, Asheville Food and Beverage United, and Buncombe Decides—keep our campaign’s efforts in front of the County Commission.

This campaign started in the spring in order to encourage the Buncombe County Tourism Development Authority (BCTDA) to devote its nascent “LIFT Fund” to fund subsidized housing construction for the region’s working people—without whom there would be no tourism economy.

Stay tuned for more news on the campaign, as the BCTDA is set to appoint the inaugural LIFT Fund committee soon. And if you aren’t one of the 2,200+ folks that have signed the coalition’s petition yet, you can find it on our website here.

Member Commentary: “Scarcity or Solidarity?”

Speaking of the campaign to lobby the BCTDA to use its money for subsidized housing construction: in our latest “member commentary” blog post, one of our members uses the Tourism Taxes for Affordable Housing campaign as a jumping off point to discuss how we can activate solidarity using multiple overlapping and complementary solutions to Asheville’s housing crisis.

You can read the blog post here.

Online Event: “Unlocking Your Impact: How to be a Pro-Housing Advocate” with YIMBY Action - Wednesday, September 20th

Asheville For All’s parent organization, YIMBY Action, regularly hosts advocacy training webinars for free, and the next one coming up is “Unlocking Your Impact: How to be a Pro-Housing Advocate.”

This event is the first of three in a series.

Here’s the description:

Your voice is an integral part of the YIMBY movement to create abundant, affordable housing for everyone. Come learn how you can take easy steps to begin your advocacy journey!

Join YIMBY Action for Unlocking Your Impact: How to be a Pro-Housing Advocate. You’ll learn about the housing shortage and how you can make an impact in your community right now.

This online event is on Wednesday, September 20th, at 7pm. Click here to RSVP.
