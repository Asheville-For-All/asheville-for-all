“Unlocking Your Impact: How to Tell Your Housing Story” with YIMBY Action
Wednesday February 21, 4:00PM

Our parent organization YIMBY Action is hosting an online event:

In the pro-housing movement, you don’t need to be a policy expert to inspire change. You can make an impact in your community by simply sharing your story and encouraging others to do the same.

Join us for our Unlocking Your Impact: How to Tell Your Housing Story training. You will learn how to share your housing story, why sharing your story is so important, and where to share your story to advocate for abundant, affordable housing in your community.
