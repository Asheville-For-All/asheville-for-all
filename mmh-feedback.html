How To Provide Feedback on Asheville’s Missing Middle Housing Initiative

On August 4, 2023, the City of Asheville and Opticos Design held an “open house” feedback session where residents could learn about the city’s missing middle initiative.

Folks that couldn’t attend the in-person event have until Friday, August 11th at 5pm to provide feedback online.

Here’s how to do it. You’ll need to have two websites up at the same time:

  • Open this link to view a PDF of the posters that were on display at the in-person event.
  • Open this link for the comments form.

You’ll complete one page of the comments form after you review each of the poster “stations” in the PDF.

Thank you for completing this form and letting the City of Asheville know that you want to see housing abundance in our core residential neighborhoods!
